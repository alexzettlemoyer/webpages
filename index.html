Welcome to my Webpage!


My name is Alex Zettlemoyer and I'm a freshman Computer Science major. I chose Computer Science because I deeply enjoy problem solving, and I feel as though this major is the rawest form of it. I think my brain truly understands code and the methods of programing with much less effort than other subjects. Furthermore, the endless possibilities of programming have always fascinated me. I know that I could become a software engineer, a game designer, or work in medicine - I chose this major because there are so many directions I could go with it.


Currently, I work as a hostess in a breakfast restaurant called 'Stack's Kitchen.' Although I wouldn't consider this as a long term job, I do genuinely enjoy it. The restaurant is almost always packed on the weekends and having up to an hour wait makes it a high-stress environment, which I usually enjoy. I only plan to have this job until I get a degree, but after that I don't necessarily have one concrete 'dream job.' I just know I would like to work somewhere in the field of Computer Science or Chemistry.
